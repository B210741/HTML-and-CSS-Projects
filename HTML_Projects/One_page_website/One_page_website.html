Lilu was found in a rubbish tip in Singapore at 8 weeks old. She was malnourished, sick, and covered in fleas.
Fortunately, she was rescued by a kind-hearted person who nursed her back to health and gave her a new home in England.

After 2 years of intensive training, she is now a qualfied therapy dog for the sober.
