This is a story about a resuced puppy named Lilu.

Lilu was found in a rubbish tip in Singapore at 8 weeks old. She was malnourished, sick, and covered in fleas. Fortunately, she was rescued by a kind-hearted person who nursed her back to health and gave her a new home in England.

Lilu now lives happily with her fur parents and new friends in a stately home in the English countryside. She has also committed to helping other people.

After 2 years of hardwork and training, she is now a qualfied therapy dog for the sober.
